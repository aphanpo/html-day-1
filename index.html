About Amy!

I am born and raised in Hawaii. I moved to Las Vegas about 3 years ago because I needed a change. I wanted to start living the "adulthood" stages of my life. I wanted to experience true freedom and learn the "hard" way to budgeting and taking care of important bills like rent.

Lessons I've learned so far:

  1. Do Something!
    2. -- Find something to do to make money. Find a job because you can't live off of savings forever.
  2. Get Comfortable with Getting Uncomfortable
    3. -- Being in a new environment calls for EXPLORING! Go out and make new connections. Las Vegas is a 24hr city so there is always something to do. There are casinos and bars all over Vegas.
  3. Adjust Your Mindset
    4. -- Having a Positive outlook on life is important. There will be many struggles while trying to adjust but staying positive will help you get through the day.

I am sure there are many life lessons I still need to learn while living in Las Vegas.
