About Me:

Many have tried to pronounce my lastname correctly and failed. It is a combination of both my parent's lastname. I am full Lao even though most people think I am Filipino. Whenever someone automatically starts talking to me in any type of Filipino language, I don't intend to be rude but I have to immediately stop them midsentence. I am born and raised in Hawaii. I moved to Las Vegas about 3 years ago because I needed a change. I wanted to start living the "adulthood" stages of my life. I wanted to experience true freedom and learn the "hard" way to budgeting and taking care of important bills like rent.

Lessons I've learned so far:
  1. Do Something!
    2. -- Find something to do to make money. Find a job because you can't live off of savings forever.
  2. Get Comfortable with Getting Uncomfortable
    3. -- Being in a new environment calls for EXPLORING! Go out and make new connections. Las Vegas is a 24hr city so there is always something to do. There are casinos and bars all over Vegas.

  3. Adjust Your Mindset
    4. -- Having a Positive outlook on life is important. There will be many struggles while trying to adjust but staying positive will help you get through the day.
I am sure there are many life lessons I still need to learn while living in Las Vegas.
